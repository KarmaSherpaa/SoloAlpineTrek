SOLO ALPINE TREK



Soloalpine Trek.

"Solo Alpine Trek and Expedition" is a trek & tour operating company located in Kathmandu (Baudha). Our office is fully equipped with all modern communication equipments and staff with dedicated team of professionals leading by Mr. Shree Dhan Rai "Chairman" who understand the critical responsibilities of handling every tour undertaken buys. Our aim is to provide the best tourism services to the maximum number of travelers visiting Nepal. Our principle is to give the best, most enjoyable and hassle-free holiday, trek or expedition at a reasonable price. Our staffs are long experience in this field allows us to avoid the common problems and pitfalls we have seen on other trips over the years. We would be happy to help you plan your holiday, or those of your clients, in the most pleasant way possible. We are always ready to provide the services you want, the way you want them, with up-front information on costs and requirements. No hidden costs, false promises, or low initial pricing with charges piled on later. You will find that in this respect alone, we are well ahead of the traditional norms. For us you are always number-one, that is why we are always flexible and accommodating. Our objective is to provide you convenience, service, and a quality experience. All our trips are arranged with the utmost attention to safety, environmental concerns, and our guest's satisfaction. All trekking staff and porters are the very best, and we make sure they are well-equipped and insured against injury or accident. Our primary products are trekking and mountaineering expeditions in the Nepal Himalayas, but we can also arrange rafting trips, sightseeing tours, jungle safaris, leisurely cultural tours, or business trips and conference arrangements. We take care of all necessary reservations, shipping, and logistical details. The Programs described are in brief. Some of the best, most popular and unique trips have been selected to give you a preview of the tours. For more information send us a fax, e-mail or letter. We are happy to receive suggestions as well. Replies are guaranteed within two days. Our company is registered at Department of Tourism & Recognized by His Majesty's Government of Nepal.
