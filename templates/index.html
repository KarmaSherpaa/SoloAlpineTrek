SOLO ALPINE TREK

"Solo Alpine Trek and Expedition" is your go-to trek and tour operator in Kathmandu, Nepal. Led by Chairman Mr. Shree Dhan Rai and a dedicated team, we offer top-notch tourism services at fair prices. With a focus on safety, environmental concerns, and guest satisfaction, we ensure hassle-free holidays, treks, and expeditions. From trekking and mountaineering to rafting trips and cultural tours, we've got you covered. Contact us for transparent pricing and personalized service, backed by our recognition from the Government of Nepal.
